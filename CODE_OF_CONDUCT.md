# Code of Conduct

Betabots should be a practical, respectful project space for people building and testing products with synthetic users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, specific, and constructive.
- Assume people may be testing fragile early products.
- Separate technical criticism from personal criticism.
- Respect privacy and do not request secrets, private user data, or production credentials in public issues.
- Keep examples synthetic unless real data is explicitly safe to publish.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, or non-consensual user data.
- Encouraging production spam, fake engagement against real users, deceptive bot traffic, or payment abuse.
- Using project spaces to evade or misrepresent the project license.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocks from project spaces.

For private conduct or safety concerns, contact the maintainer through the security reporting path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
